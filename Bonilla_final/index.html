What are they?

Kaiju is a Japanese term that is commonly associated with media involving giant monsters. A subgenre of science-fiction, more precisely monster films, its widespread contemporary use is credited to tokusatsu (special effects) director Eiji Tsuburaya and filmmaker Ishirō Honda, who popularized the kaiju film genre by creating the Godzilla franchise and its spin-offs. The term can also refer to the monsters themselves, which are usually depicted attacking major cities, and battling either the military or other creatures. Kaiju are monstrous creatures that come in different sizes and shapes. All of them have different features, some more humanoid than others. Every kaiju possess a Core (核, Kaku), which is their vital organ and weakness, inside their body, and since every kaiju comes in a different type the location of the core changes accordingly.

Fun Fact!

Every Kaiju is given a Fortitude level (フォルティチュード, Forutichūdo) which is a way for the Defense Force to measure the creature's power and its potential threat to humanity.[5] According to the Fortitude level, Kaiju are then classified power-wise.

Abilities

All kaiju gain their powers from their Uni-organ (ユニ器官, Yuni-kikan), an organ containing their specific powers. Despite their common nature, each kaiju possesses different abilities and powers, so it's quite rare to find similar kaiju. Some powers most kaiju have in common are super strength, proliferation abilities, and great fortitude levels. Some of them are able to fly[1] and the most complex are even able to practice human language and, through that, can talk.

Types of Kaijus

Yoju (余よ獣じゅう, Yojū, lit. "Residual Beast") are smaller kaiju who accompany their Honju. There are usually numerous Yoju to assist a Honju, and some kind of Yoju can have proliferative organs as well. Yoju can appear in many ways, by traveling routes made by the Honju, latching onto the Honju, being created by the Honju, and more. Some Yoju can have a fortitude number above 6.0, thus being a Honju-level threat while still being a Yoju.

Honju (本ホン獣じゅう, Honjū, lit. "Main Beast") are usually the biggest and most powerful kaiju, and the one who generates and guides all the other Yoju through their proliferative organs. Their fortitude level is usually above 6.0. A Honju with a fortitude level of 6.4 would require an entire Platoon of Defense Force soldiers to defeat it, and only a Captain or Vice-Captain could defeat one alone.

Daikaiju (大だい怪かい獣じゅう, Daikaijū, lit. "Large Monster") is a special category of kaiju. It is the name given to particularly strong kaiju, whose fortitude level exceeds the number 8.0. Having appeared ever since the Meireki era, these kinds of kaiju are part of the strongest category, with their power not necessarily being linked to their size, since Kafka, who has a fortitude level of 9.8 in his kaiju form, the highest seen thus far in the series, defeated multiple kaiju much larger than him in size. Due to their incredible power, Daikaiju can also be Honju and guide their group of Yoju (like Kaiju No. 10 with the Preondactyl Kaiju).[10] In spite of that, Daikaiju are not above operating individually on their own. According to Isao Shinomiya, the Defense Force presently has around two dozen elite officers who stand a chance at taking on a Daikaiju, less than ten in single-combat and when fighting a numbered Daikaiju, the number gets even lower.

Identified Kaiju (識しき別べつ怪かい獣じゅう, Shikibetsu Kaijū) are daikaiju that are extremely powerful and have a fortitude level of 9.0 or higher. Even if their fortitude level is lower than 9.0 (like Kaiju No. 9, who had a fortitude level of 8.5 before shedding its skin), if there is a need for identification, then a codename will be given. While daikaiju of this caliber have always appeared in history, their identification numbers were implemented only after the fortitude observation system was established.

Fun Fact!

Kaiju has been theorized as being born from the immense energy produced by the faults in the earth, acting upon the underground substances, plants, and animals to produce them.[36] It was further elaborated as the Mysterious Larva was born from the "Dragon Veins" deep within the earth after the hearts of the warriors formed a core

The Kaiju themselves vary wildly in size, anywhere from 20 to 500 feet. Their bodies are typically an amalgam of reptilian, cephalopod and ichthyological traits. Nearly all Kaiju observed to date are pentaradially symmetrical, with body parts that typically come in pairs instead coming in sets of five.

The Kaiju have been observed to be highly aggressive, attacking most other entities on sight as well as frequently battling each other. Although spectacular, these fights are rarely fatal as Kaiju possess both supernatural durability and regenerative abilities. They also possess a variety of offensive thaumaturgical abilities as well, such as being able to exhale spellfire that does not extinguish underwater.

The exact population of Kaiju is unknown, but as they have yet to deplete their primary food source, it appears that the Kaiju kill significantly fewer whales than Humans did during the era of industrial whaling. Given both the size and aggressive nature of the Kaiju, this is somewhat perplexing.

In addition to the problem of food, the colossal size of the Kaiju clearly violates the square-cube law, as well as introducing a variety of other scale related problems. In addition to possessing extensive non-Euclidean biology and abnormally thaumaturgically active cells, the Kaiju have a relatively low internal Hume value, lessening the effect of our reality's laws of physics on its body.
